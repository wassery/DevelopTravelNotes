## 环境
> 系统：Windows 10
> 虚拟机：VMware-workstation-full-10.0.2-1744117.1415342309
> https://my.vmware.com/zh/web/vmware/downloads

> http://tw.archive.ubuntu.com/ubuntu-cd/xenial/ubuntu-16.04.5-server-amd64.iso
